About Antonio

Antonio's Restaurant was founded by Antonio Estevez in 1986. Before he got into the restaurant business, he was an immigrant coming from Mexico with his Mom and two sisters. Antonio was looking for a job to provide himself and his family. So, he decided to work as a bus-boy at a restuarant called Te Amor. After two years of being a bus-boy, his manager wanted to try him out as cook, since the recent moved to a another Mexican restaurant. After Antonio's first try of cooking, the manger was impressed. So, the manager decided to move him as a cook. After a few years of recongnition on being a head chef at Te Amor, he decided to run his own business venture. After his restaurant opened, it became a huge hit. From his famous burrios to yoummy desserts, Antonio's Restaurant is the place to go.
